Egg Fried Rice Recipe

What is an Egg Fried Rice?

It is a rice dish that originated from China and is almost as simple as it sounds.But simple doesn't mean it tastes bad,in fact when made properly it makes a certain uncle somewhere in the world go Fuiyoh!!!!.Although it originated from china it's popular all over asia and is a signature dish in many countries.Some varieties of fried rice are Yangzhou fried rice,Hokkien fried rice (both are from China) and Nasi goreng (from Indonesia).I'm sure you are hungry to devour some fried rice now so lets get started

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of water
  • 2 Tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of salt
  • MSG
  • Day old rice
  • Oil
  • 1/2 Onion finely chopped
  • 1/2 Cup of green beans/peas
  • 1 Large egg finely beaten
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of black pepper

Steps

  1. Bring water, soy sauce, and salt to a boil in a medium saucepan. Stir in instant rice and remove from heat. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a medium skillet or wok over medium heat. Saut√© onions and green beans in hot oil for 2 to 3 minutes. Pour in beaten egg and fry for 2 minutes, scrambling egg while it cooks.
  3. Add cooked rice to egg mixture; mix well. Season with pepper
  4. sprinkle some MSG while it's cooking

And there you have it your egg fried rice is ready
