Biryani Recipe

What is a Biryani?

Biryani is a rice dish mixed with several spices and meat(chicken,beef,mutton).It is a very filling and delicious dish .The origins of this dish is highly debatable as some sources say it was developed in the royal kitchen of the Mughal Empire and some say it was made in India or Pakistan.Biryani is very popular in Souther Asia.It is a incredibly delicious dish that is used during wedding and other function.It is very popular in countries like India and Pakistan and over the years have developed many flavours world wide.It is one of the most popular dishes from India

It is made differently in every part of the world .There are different types of biryani based on where they are made in.Even in India different parts of country make the dish differently and use different ingredients.Each state in India has a different take on biryani.We will be looking at a generalized and simple version of this dish which will provide u with the accurate flavours and let u experience this wonderful dish at home

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 Tablespoon of oil
  • 3 Tablespoon plain yogurt
  • 2 Tablespoon distilled white vinegar
  • 1 onion slice
  • 2 Tomatoes chopped
  • 2 Green chile peppers
  • 2 Cloves of garlic crushed
  • 1 Tablespoon of ginger paste
  • 2 Tablespoon garam masala
  • 1 Tablespoon dried mint
  • Salt and pepper
  • 5 Sprigs of cilantro
  • 1 Whole chicken skin removed and cut into pieces
  • 1 Liter of water
  • 4 cups of uncooked basmati rice
  • 1 Cinnamon stick
  • 1 Bay leaf
  • 4 Pods of green cardamom
  • 1 Pod black cardamom
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of saffron food coloring

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat.Stir in yogurt and vinegar.Add onion,cook and stir until golden brown.Add tomatoes,green chile,garlic and ginger paste.Cook and stir until tomatoes are tender.Season with garam masala,salt,mint and pepper.Stir in cilantro

  2. Place chicken into the mixture in the skillet.Reduce heat to medium-low,cover and continue cooking,stirring occasionally,until,chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear,about 45 minutes.An instant-read thermometer inserted into the chicken should show at least 74 degrees C (165 degrees F)

  3. Meanwhile,bring water and rice to a boil in a large pot.Mix in cinnamon stick,bay leaf,green cardamom and black cardamom.Season with salt.Cover with lid and reduce heat to low and simmer until rice is tender and water is absorbed,about 20 minutes.Discard cinnamon stick,bay leaf and cardamom

  4. Spread 1/2 of the cooked rice in the bottom of a large serving dish. Pour chicken mixture on top. Cover with remaining cooked rice. Sprinkle with saffron food coloring, mix well

Serve and enjoy your delicious biryani right at home
